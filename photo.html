My favorite Stockholm photo spots

My starting point is usually the "Red Boat". It's a nice spot catching a summer sunset, and the view you can see from there is similar to this one. It's of course impossible to guarantee a show such as this one, but May and June are good months for watching a sunset from this spot. Access to water is completely unprotected in this area, so please take care:
